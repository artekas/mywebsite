GAEA AI: Ice Demand Forecasting

Project Brief

The project was prompted by the 2022 heatwave in Spain, which led to a national ice shortage, lost revenue, and public backlash. The business problem we were addressing is how to better forecast and respond to climate-sensitive demand for ice products

Objectives

The analysis supported several key business objectives:

  • Understanding service utilization trends and patterns
  • Assessing staff and capacity adequacy across regions
  • Reducing missed appointments through targeted interventions
  • Evaluating the value of external data sources for decision-making

Conclusion

The project uncovered meaningful trends in missed appointments and regional resource disparities. These insights informed recommendations for strategic planning, service delivery improvements, and evidence-based staffing decisions.
